Panther Power Workout Plan

Here at Panther Power you can find someone to help you build a workout routine for a variety of different things, ranging from powerlifting to kickboxing
Here is an example workout routine focusing mainly on calisthenics

Ab Day

4 Sets in total

- Sit-ups x 15
- Leg raises x 15
- In and Outs x 15
- Russian twists x 30 (15 each side)
1 minute break
- Twist mountain climbers x 60
- Bicycles x 60
- Plank x 1 min
- Side Planks x 1 min each side
2 minute break

Arm Day

4 Sets in total

- Bicep curls x 15 each arm (20 lbs)
- Shoulder press x 15 (20 lbs)
- Tricep row x 15 (25 lbs)
1 minute break
- Chest press x 15 (50 lbs)
- Back pull down x 15 (70 lbs)
- Outside forearm curl x 15 (60 lbs)
- Inside forearm curl x 30 (60 lbs)
2 minute break

Leg Day

4 Sets in total

- Squats x 15 (20 lbs)
- Lunges x 16 (8 on each side) (20 lbs)
- Calf raises x 30 (25 lbs)
- Jumping jacks x 30
1 minute break
- Sumo squats x 15 (20 lbs)
- Hamstring pull downs x 15 (50 lbs)
- Leg press x 15 (70 lbs)
2 minute break
Cool down: Incline jog on treadmill for 1/2 mile

Cardio Day

3 Sets in total

- Jog 3/4 mile on treadmill, then walk 1/4 mile, then jog another 1/4 mile
- Light calf walk & Knee to palms
- Burpees x 25
- Hell raisers x 15
1 minute break
- High knees x 1 min
- Eliptical jogging stride x 10 min
- Bicycle x 5 miles
2 minute break

Rec website: https://www.pvamu.edu/recsports/src/
