Diet

  1. Eat in a calorie deficit.
  2. Try to eat your current body weight in grams of protein. Ex: 160 pounds = 160 grams of protein.
  3. Make sure you drink atleast 2 bottles of water 2 hours before working out to lower dehydraytion risk.
  4. A Web site you can find the amount of calories you shoul eat: Calorie Intake Calculator

Summary

  • Eat in a calorie deficit more and more protein for an athletic look and muscle growth.
  • Workout 4 times a week according to your schedule.
  • Drink a decent amount of water and sleep atleast 7-8 hours a night to maximize energy.
