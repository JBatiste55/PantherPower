What is RPE? RPE is a way to measure intensity during an exercise on a scale of 10. For example when hitting an exercise at an RPE of 3 when you finish the required reps you should still be able to do 7 reps past that. In conclusion, when completing an exercise at an RPE you would complete the required reps at a weight or intensity where if you had to do more reps it would be the 10 subtracted by the RPE level
